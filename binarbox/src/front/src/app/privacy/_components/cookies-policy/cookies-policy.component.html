COOKIE POLICY
About cookies

Cookies are small text files that contain letters and numbers that are installed on your device when you visit a website. From a utility point of view, this file allows you to remember your settings and preferences when you visit the site again, to give you a better online experience and leads to a better traffic analysis on a site, accessing pages.

You have the possibility to allow cookies or not, through the settings made in the browsers as well as through the cookie banner on this site.

Cookies cannot be used to run programs or transmit computer viruses to your device, they are "passive" files in the sense that they do not contain software programs, viruses or spyware and cannot access information from your device.

The data collected by using cookies are used both to facilitate certain functionalities for the user but also to ensure visibility in accessing and using the services for the administrator of the website, with the user's consent.

Types of cookies and the purpose of their use on our site

We use two types of cookies :

  • session cookies – active during a browsing session and they remain until the browser is closed
  • persistent cookies – active for a longer period of time. The persistent ones remain on the terminal a period that is determined by certain parameters or until they are explicitly deleted by user

Cookies can be categorized by their source:

  • first-party cookies – are placed on our website
  • third-party cookies – are placed by a third party other than the website owner ( usually through a social network, advertiser, electronic payment services)

The site www.lockbridges.com uses cookies for certain basic activities and for analyzing the performance of our site, to give visitors a better browsing experience.

Thereby, cookies can also be categorized from the perspective of the purpose for which they are used:

  • strictly Necessary – Required for good navigation, functionality, and security of our website that may be affected by disabling them. These cannot be disabled by the user
  • preferences and statistics - are useful in analyzing visitors, pages accessed, all data being used to optimize the functionality of our site and online experience. Using software from the Google suite (e.g. Google Analytics), these can be disabled by the user
  • marketing cookies (advertising) - are used to retain your profile (parameters, settings, etc.) and to display relevant advertising. These can be disabled by the user
