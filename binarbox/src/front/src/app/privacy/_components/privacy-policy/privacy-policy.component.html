Overview

According to applicable legislation for the protection of personal data (GDPR -EU Regulation 2016/679) and applicable national law, we present below Privacy Policy regarding the processing of your personal data, as a visitor of our website www.lockbridges.com.

Context

In this context, according to the regulation mentioned below, as a visitor and user of our website, you hold the quality of the data subject, and we, the quality of processor (according to GDPR terminology), which collects, stores and processes personal data.   • IP

  • browser type and settings used
  • type of operating system used
  • the time spent on the website
  • browsing website pages and preferences
  • transfer speeds.

The legal bases of processing personal data are:

  • your consent (e.g. processing from cookies)
  • our legitimate interest (e.g. automatic data collection)

We keep your personal data for a period that is not greater than the one required to fulfill the purpose mentioned above, according to internal polices and any applicable legislation.

Only if necessary, we will transmit your data to various authorities, public institutions, attorneys, insurance companies, auditors, external consultants - only insofar as they need to be known or processed according to the applicable legal requirements, including when we exercise our right to defense in the face of complaints / disputes. Your personal data does not leave EU space as a standard, and when it does we make sure that there are adequate protections (suitability decisions, Privacy Schield, etc.).

Data security is one of our important goals, covering topics such as confidentiality, integrity and availability. Through well-established organizational and technical measures, we ensure that your data is protected.
